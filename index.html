Sylvan Springs History Project

I took notice that a lot of history seems to be fading away from the internet. From the age-old AOL, to Geocities, historic digital resources are disappearing, to be never seen again. This is my effort to preserve the history of Sylvan Springs, as a long term project. I will work directly with the town and others, if they want to receive periodic mirrors of this info, for long term storage.
